ABOUT

Scarlett Lingwood is a visual artist who is living and working in New York City. She moved here in late August 2014 to complete her first masters at The School Of Visual Arts in Fine Arts and is now working towards completing her second masters in Integrated Media Arts at Hunter College. In the year after she completed her masters, she founded and co manages Fruit Exchange Studio Collective. Scarlett completed her foundation at Camberwell College Of Arts and her undergraduate degree at Goldsmiths college in Fine Arts. For Scarletts degree she received the Exposure 13 award from the Parasol Unit.
