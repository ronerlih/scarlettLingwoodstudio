Brain


Untitled, Photograph, 2013



Untitled, 64 drawing installation, 2013

NW8 – SE8 London Exposure 13 Award Audio Extract 5 minutes 01 second duration of piece 3 hours 54 minutes 14 seconds.

Brain began as a idea after a unexpected encounter where a stranger remarked to me. “Hello, you look lost.” Despite the fact I knew exactly where I was coming from and where I was going. These words made me feel on diversion.
